The Incident Reporting and Tracking Center (IRTC) is a web-based application designed to streamline the process of logging, managing, and tracking incidents within an organization or community. Whether it's workplace safety issues, IT disruptions, or public complaints, IRTC provides a centralized platform where users can report incidents in real-time. The system features role-based access, automated status updates, incident categorization, and visual dashboards for administrators to monitor patterns and response times. With built-in notifications and analytics, IRTC enhances accountability, improves response efficiency, and helps organizations maintain transparency and safety across all operations.
